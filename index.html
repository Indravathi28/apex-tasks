Bottle Gourd Halwa (Lauki Halwa)

A simple Indian dessert made with grated bottle gourd, milk, sugar, and ghee.

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 25–30 mins Serves: 4 Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated bottle gourd
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3–1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10–12 cashews & almonds
  • 1 tbsp raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat ghee and fry dry fruits.
  2. Add grated lauki and sauté.
  3. Pour milk and cook until soft.
  4. Add sugar & cardamom, cook till thick.
  5. Mix nuts and serve warm.

Tips & Variations

  • Add khoya or condensed milk for richness.
  • Use tender green lauki.
  • Adjust sugar to taste.
